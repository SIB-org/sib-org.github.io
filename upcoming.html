SIB MUN 2

Date: Beginning of Term 2 (Tentative) • Time: Activity Period (Tentative) Venue: The Bishop’s Co-Ed School, Undri

A fully fledged MUN conference right in our school. The SIB MUN Initiative will consist of multiple sessions held in the activity period throughout conference week. Every student gets to participate and represent their country. It fosters skills in public speaking, debate, diplomacy, conflict resolution, negotiation, and more.
